Hello, my name is Lourdes. I'm 22 years old, I live in Rosario, Argentina, where I studied fashion design at ISCV (Instituto Superior de Comunicación Visual). Passionate about uniting different branches of design and art in the same concept, I find myself observing the details in space, day by day, enjoying the fusion between my observing mind and physical reality. I really like to explore in abstraction, and in this way materialize concepts in tangible facts: clothing. Always ready to observe the new in the ordinary, I seek to express a message through design on the body, a message of unity.
